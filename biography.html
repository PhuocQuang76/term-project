ABOUT MYSELF

I am Phuoc Quang, born in Ho Chi Minh City, VietNam. I live in California with my husband for 5 years. It was my dream to work oversea and decided to move to Tokyo, Japan after graduating from college. While in Japan I studied their language and culture afterwords worked in information technology for various companies. Tokyo is a great place to live. However, I felt alone and wanted to live with family. So I decided to visit my sister in Los Angeles, USA and a month later I met a man who became my husband and we have been together for five years. Having seen people worked in computer field, I have decided to become a better software engineer specializing in Web Application development. That decision led me to Boston University.
